A NOVEL SVM BASED DECODER FOR IMAGE CAPTIONING REMOTE SENSING

Welcome to the Image Captioning Project

A novel SVM- based decoder for remote sensing image captioning

Project Information:

Most remote sensing image captioning models rely on CNN-RNN frameworks. RNN-based frameworks need a large dataset and significant computational resources. This article introduces a novel decoder using Support Vector Machines (SVM) instead of RNNs. SVM-based decoder is efficient, especially with limited training data. Experiments on four datasets confirm its ability to produce meaningful image descriptions. It offers faster training and inference times compared to other models.
